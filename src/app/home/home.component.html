About Us

Food banks are non-profit organizations created to distribute donated food directly to individuals or to agencies that feed needy individually.

Food banks provide an important function in community by targeting a small, low income, subset of the local population.

The initiative to start the Food Bank was from Team "Food Donor" from the regular items being dropped off in the cupboards located in mosques by anonymous donors. "Food Donor" was created mainly to serve the marginalised community by offering assistance with basic household necessities.

Food that can be donated

"Food Donor" is a platform as an emergency aid provider for those who need or discard food supplies due to the narrowing of life. "Food Donor" provide the initiative that aimed at providing basic foodstuff, such as rice, canned sardines, sugar, salt, soya sauce, cooking oil and flour.
